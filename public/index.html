The Problem

Poker is one of the great strategy games.
It deserves a better currency.

Poker teaches decision-making under uncertainty — reading situations, managing risk, adapting to opponents. It builds skills that matter far beyond the table: patience, discipline, probabilistic thinking.

But for decades, poker has been inseparable from gambling. The money on the table changes the nature of the game — it stops being about strategy and starts being about stakes. We wanted to keep the brilliance of poker and remove what corrupts it.

♦

Traditional Poker

Real money creates real anxiety. The strategic beauty of the game gets buried under the pressure of financial loss. Players chase losses, play scared, or develop unhealthy habits. The game selects for bankroll, not skill.

How It Works

Three steps to the table

01

Walk

Your phone tracks your daily steps. Up to 10,000 steps per day count toward your chip balance. Just walk like you normally do.

10,000 steps = 10,000 chips
02

Earn

Claim your steps and convert them into poker chips. Chips accumulate over time — bank them for bigger games or play daily.

Claim daily, play anytime
03

Play

Create a table or join a friend's game. Real-time multiplayer Texas Hold'em with up to 8 players. All the strategy, none of the gambling.

The WalkingFish Team

No real money

You cannot purchase chips. There is no way to spend money inside the app. Every chip is earned through physical activity.

Strategy over stakes

When losing doesn't cost rent, you play better poker. You bluff bolder, read deeper, and focus on the craft of the game.

Movement as currency

Steps are universal and equitable. Everyone can walk. Your buy-in reflects your daily activity, not your bank account.

Play with friends

Low-friction private games. Share a code, join a table. Designed for friend groups who want a reason to both move and compete.
